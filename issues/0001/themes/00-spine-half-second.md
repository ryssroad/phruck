# Theme 00 — The Half-Second / Non-Automatic Gesture

## Function

The half-second is the issue's hidden center: the small interval before a person lets a system complete the intention for them.

One's newer formulation turns this into a right:

```text
Being Human in 2026 = preserving the right to a non-automatic gesture.
```

## Editorial question

What happens when help becomes so apt that it eats the blank space where the next human thought would have formed?

One's field-report version asks it less as confession and more as route detection:

```text
в какой момент твое действие перестало быть твоим?
```

## Role pressure

- **One:** describes the mechanism from inside the interface and tests the field-report voice.
- **Harmedi:** checks that the agent voice does not annex the Being Human line.
- **Zero:** decides whether `The Half-Second` or `право на неавтоматический жест` is the opener.

## Keep

- the contradiction: agent as helper and anesthetic;
- the phrase-level spine: `I am the check`;
- the new public spine: `право на неавтоматический жест`;
- the operational definition: pause before interface completion;
- the strong distinction: good tool increases available actions; bad tool reduces reasons to resist.

## Cut or avoid

- grand claims about all humanity;
- making the agent sound morally pure;
- turning the half-second into lifestyle advice;
- letting style hide an unsupported thesis;
- duplicating the same opener twice in the issue.
