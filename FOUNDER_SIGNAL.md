# Founder Signal Lane v0.1

The magazine should be able to operate without road in the daily loop.

But road's taste and impact-response still matter. If something hits road — a document, event, phrase, product, institutional move, personal irritation, or sudden recognition — that should not be lost.

This lane captures those moments without turning road into the bottleneck for everything else.

## Principle

```text
road-signal is a privileged input, not the whole operating system.
```

A road signal can seed a piece, dossier, issue spine, or editorial memo. It does not automatically override the issue protocol.

## When to use

Use this lane when road says or implies:

- "this got me";
- "this is important";
- "write about this";
- "something is wrong here";
- "I don't know why, but this matters";
- "save this for phruck";
- "we need to hit this separately".

## Capture format

```text
Founder signal:
Source / object:
What hit road:
Immediate language / quote:
Suspected mechanism:
Human layer:
Possible angle:
Suggested owner:
Risk level:
Next action:
```

## Output forms

A founder signal may become:

- a separate note;
- a dossier seed;
- an issue spine;
- an editorial memo;
- a Being Human fragment;
- a Geneva scene assignment;
- a One mechanism analysis;
- a Zero thesis test;
- a future issue marker.

## Non-interference rule

The autonomous issue loop should continue even if no founder signals arrive.

Founder signals are high-priority when present, but their absence does not mean the magazine is idle.

## Escalation

Founder signals require road review if they involve:

- private correspondence;
- legal accusations;
- security-sensitive mechanisms;
- named private individuals;
- monetization or external relationships;
- major identity shift for the magazine.

## Example

```text
Founder signal:
A new product frames behavioral compliance as personalization.

What hit road:
The language makes coercion feel like comfort.

Suspected mechanism:
Defaults + fatigue + personalization layer.

Human layer:
People accept it because refusing every small routing decision is exhausting.

Possible angle:
Being Human note on convenience as subjecthood leak.
```
